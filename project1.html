business problem

Echo Ceramic's current class selection process doesn't include all the information users hope to find while shopping for classes, such as teacher bios and descriptions of what they will learn. Additionally the checkout process is handled by a 3rd party, which takes customers away from the main site- making for a jarring experience.

our goal

Our goal is to provide users with the information they need to be confident with their class purchase, booking with ease, as well as a seamless and site branded checkout flow.
